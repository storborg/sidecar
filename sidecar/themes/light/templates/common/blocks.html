What qualifies as commercial use?

If you are deriving any monetary gain whatsoever from use of the images or something connected to use of the images, it's commercial. Commercial use includes, but is not limited to the following scenarios:

  • A blog or website that contains paid advertising
  • A blog or website that is owned by a company rather than an individual
  • A presentation that promotes a product or service

If you're not sure whether or not your particular usage qualifies as commercial, please go ahead and ask.
